Lima, Peru

Lima is the capital and largest city of Peru, and one of the most important urban centers in South America. Lima, Peru, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and delicious cuisine. It's a city where you can find ancient Incan ruins alongside colonial architecture, and experience a blend of traditional and modern influences in its food, art, and nightlife. Lima is also a major transportation hub, often serving as a gateway to other popular Peruvian destinations like Machu Picchu.
